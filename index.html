Ayanat Berdygali

Photographer & Graphic Designer

Photography has escalated from being just a hobby for me to a future career. This can be a little scary, putting something you’re most passionate about in a line of work, but sometimes I think it makes it more thrilling. Don’t get me wrong, there are still times of stress, doubt, and even failure, but that’s what builds me up to be the photographer that I am. Through these times, I’ll still observe and look for these moments that define what my true love for photography is, and fall in love all over again.
